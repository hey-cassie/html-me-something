Lemmy the Chihuahua

Lemmy's Rescue Story

In late 2017, a little nameless chihuahua puppy was rescued from a shelter in Cuba, Missouri. He was scheduled to be euthanized the following day. On February 17, 2018, by a wild stroke of luck, he was adopted by Cassie & Peat! The three have been best buds ever since.

Five Fun Facts about Lemmy

  • He's tiny!
  • He loves to snooze!
  • He loves going on bike rides!
  • He's bossy!
  • His best friend is a pitbull named Bruce!

Lemmy's Namesake

Lemmy the chihuahua is named after Lemmy Kilmister, the lead singer of Motörhead, and rock-n-roll legend who played by his own rules. Both Cassie & Peat are big fans!
