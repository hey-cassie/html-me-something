Lemmy the Chihuahua

Lemmy's Rescue Story

In late 2017, a little nameless Chihuahua puppy was rescued from a shelter in Cuba, Missouri. He was scheduled to be euthanized the following day. On February 17, 2018, by a wild stroke of luck, he was adopted by Cassie & Peat! The three have been best buds ever since.

Five Fun Facts about Lemmy

  • He's tiny! (Only 7 lbs!)
  • He loves to snooze!
  • He loves going on bike rides!
  • He's bossy!
  • His best friend is a pitbull named Bruce!

Lemmy's Namesake

Lemmy the Chihuahua is named after Lemmy Kilmister, the founder and lead singer of Motörhead. Lemmy is a rock 'n roll legend, who played by his own rules, and both Cassie & Peat are big fans.

Trick Question: Lemmy IS God!

In the 1994 comedy, Airheads, in a now well-known scene, a band of misfit musicians challenge a stranger to a little rock ’n roll trivia. True to form, they ask, “Who would win in a wrestling match: Lemmy or God?” While the man blurts out the wrong answer, the group exclaims it’s a trick question because “Lemmy is God.” The phrase "Lemmy is God" has stuck with Motörhead fans, and for this reason, Cassie & Peat lovingly use the tag #lemmyisgod when referencing Lemmy the Chihuahua on social media.
